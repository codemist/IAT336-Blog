Sahil Sran

Product Designer

Hardworking, thoughtful, and aspiring to make a name for himself. Sahil is a 4th year IAT student looking to dive into game design post-graduation. Though he doesn't have an eye for physical prototyping or product design, he does excel in creative ideation in the form of sketching, both digitally and physically. His proposed designs attempt to reflect objects from global cultures, such as his Japanese Lantern and Accordian inspired webcams.
